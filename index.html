About Me

I am an ambitious **Electrical Engineering PhD candidate** at the University of Kansas with a deep specialization in **Silicon Photonics** and high-speed electro-optical systems. My background blends rigorous academic research with practical industry experience gained during co-op and internship roles at leading technology firms like **Nokia** and **Openlight Photonics**.

My core expertise lies in the full lifecycle of photonic component development: from **Verilog** and **PCB design (Cadence)** for hardware integration, to advanced simulation using **MATLAB/Simulink**, and hands-on validation using complex test equipment like **Optical Spectrum Analyzers** and **Network Analyzers**.

I am actively seeking full-time R&D or Hardware Engineering roles where I can leverage my expertise in optical communications, high-speed testing, and data analysis to drive innovation.
